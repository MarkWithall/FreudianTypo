Jack In Wonderland (2012)
Black comedy following the misadventures of a time traveller obsessed with the idea that Lewis Carroll was Jack the Ripper. One night the traveller accidentally runs down his suspect in a coach and cripples him. In a state of panic he decides that it is necessary to take over the role of the Ripper to avoid changing the future. Our protagonist endeavours to recreate the crimes as best he can using modern medical text books, Internet references and a photocopier to reproduce letters taunting the police. But he will find out that murdering prostitutes in the Victorian fog of London is not as easy as it first appears.
